Welcome to Stacked Deli, Sheffield's freshest and most exciting sandwich shop, where we've redefined the art of sandwich making. Located in Sheffield, Stacked serves up mouthwatering creations on freshly baked focaccia that will leave your taste buds begging for more.

Our Mission

At Stacked Deli, our mission is simple: to craft sandwiches that are stacked high with premium ingredients and bursting with flavor. We believe in the power of a well-crafted sandwich, and we've made it our duty to bring you an exceptional experience with every bite.

Freshly Baked Focaccia

The heart and soul of our sandwiches is our freshly baked focaccia, creating the perfect canvas for our culinary creations. The result? A sandwich that's not just a meal; it's an experience.

Quality Ingredients

We're passionate about quality, which is why we source the finest, locally-sourced ingredients. From our crisp, garden-fresh vegetables to our handpicked meats and cheeses, every element of your sandwich is carefully selected to ensure the highest standard of taste and freshness.

Creative Flavors

We take sandwich creativity to the next level, offering a menu filled with innovative combinations and classic favorites. Whether you're a meat lover, a vegetarian, or a vegan, our diverse selection guarantees a delectable sandwich for every palate.

Stay Updated

Keep up with our latest offerings, daily specials, and exciting updates by following us on social media. We can't wait to share our passion for sandwiches with you!
